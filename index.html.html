💧 Daily Habits

  • Drink 80–100 oz water daily
  • Sleep 7–8 hours minimum

  • Avoid liquid calories / soda

🤝 Partner Rules

  • Never miss a Monday

🥩 Weekly Meal Prep

High protein · Low carb · Made for two · Prepped every Sunday

📅
Sunday is Prep Day

Set aside 2–2.5 hours every Sunday. Batch cook proteins, pre-portion meals. This eliminates daily decision fatigue and keeps you on track all week.

🥚
Egg & Sausage Muffin Cups
10 min prep · 22 min cook · 12 muffins
~28g protein~2g carbs~18g fat~280 cal
Whisk 10 eggs with salt, pepper & hot sauce. Brown 1 lb breakfast sausage. Grease muffin tin, fill with sausage, pour egg over, top with cheddar. Bake 375°F for 20–22 min. Microwave 60 sec each morning.
🧀 Cheese🥚 EggsFreezer-friendly
🍳
Bacon & Cheese Egg Bake
8 min prep · 30 min cook · 8 servings
~26g protein~2g carbs~22g fat~310 cal
Cook 1 lb bacon, crumble into greased 9×13 dish. Whisk 12 eggs with ½ cup heavy cream. Pour over bacon, top with shredded cheddar. Bake 350°F for 28–30 min. Slice into squares.
🥓 Bacon🧀 CheeseMeal prep hero
🌯
Scrambled Egg & Sausage Bowls
5 min prep · 12 min cook · 5 servings
~32g protein~3g carbs~20g fat~325 cal
Brown 1 lb sausage with diced bell peppers and onion. Scramble 10 eggs separately. Combine in meal prep containers, top with shredded cheese. Reheat covered 90 seconds.
🌭 Sausage🥚 Eggs5-day supply
💡 Pro Tip: Make 2 different breakfasts on Sunday — rotate Mon/Wed/Fri and Tue/Thu so you don't eat the same thing two days in a row.
🍗
Sheet Pan Herb Chicken Thighs
10 min prep · 35 min cook · 8 servings
~38g protein~1g carbs~14g fat~280 cal
Toss 8 chicken thighs with olive oil, garlic powder, Italian seasoning. Roast at 425°F for 35 min. Pair with roasted broccoli or green beans on the same pan.
🍗 ChickenSheet pan
🥩
Taco Ground Beef Bowls
5 min prep · 15 min cook · 5 servings
~36g protein~4g carbs~20g fat~340 cal
Brown 2 lbs ground beef with taco seasoning. Top with cheddar, sour cream, diced tomatoes. Serve over shredded lettuce or cauliflower rice.
🥩 Beef🧀 Cheese
🥗
Bacon Chicken Caesar Jars
15 min prep · No cook · 5 jars
~40g protein~5g carbs~18g fat~340 cal
Layer in mason jars: Caesar dressing, sliced chicken, crumbled bacon, parmesan, romaine on top. Seal and fridge up to 4 days. Shake to dress when ready.
🍗 Chicken🥓 BaconNo reheat
💡 Pro Tip: Grill 4–5 lbs chicken Sunday — use half for lunch salads and half for dinners. One cook, multiple meals.
🥩
Slow Cooker Chuck Roast
10 min prep · 8 hrs low · 8 servings
~42g protein~3g carbs~18g fat~340 cal
Season 3–4 lb chuck roast, sear in hot pan. Add to slow cooker with beef broth, onion, bay leaf. Cook low 8 hours. Shred with forks. Freezes perfectly.
🥩 BeefSlow cookerFreezer-friendly
🍗
Cheesy Stuffed Chicken
15 min prep · 30 min cook · 4 servings
~48g protein~2g carbs~16g fat~340 cal
Butterfly chicken breasts, stuff with bacon and cream cheese + cheddar. Sear 3 min/side, finish in oven at 400°F for 20 min. Serve with sautéed spinach.
🍗 Chicken🥓 Bacon🧀 Cheese

Bunless Bacon Cheeseburgers
10 min prep · 15 min cook · 8 patties
~36g protein~1g carbs~28g fat~400 cal
Mix 2 lbs 80/20 beef with Worcestershire, garlic powder. Form 8 patties, cook on cast iron. Top with cheese and bacon. Serve in lettuce wraps.

Sausage & Pepper Skillet
10 min prep · 20 min cook · 6 servings
~28g protein~6g carbs~22g fat~330 cal
Brown sliced smoked sausage. Sauté bell peppers, onions, garlic. Return sausage, add broth and seasonings. Simmer 5 min. Finish with mozzarella.

Hard Boiled Eggs
2 min prep · 12 min cook · Dozen at a time
~12g protein~1g carbs~10g fat~140 cal
Boil a dozen every Sunday. Cold water → boil → off heat → cover 12 min → ice bath. Store in shell, fridge 7 days. The most underrated meal prep item there is.

~20g protein~1g carbs~14g fat~210 cal
Lay deli turkey or ham flat. Place string cheese in center, roll up tight. Pre-roll 10–12, store in zip bag. Ready in 30 seconds.

Bacon-Wrapped Chicken Bites
10 min prep · 25 min cook · ~24 bites
~22g protein~1g carbs~12g fat~200 cal
Cut chicken into 1-inch cubes. Wrap each in half-strip bacon, toothpick. Season with garlic, black pepper. Bake 400°F 22–25 min until crispy.

~24g protein~2g carbs~16g fat~245 cal
